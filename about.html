About PP-Planning

PP-Planning was founded with a clear mission: to demystify the planning application process and make it accessible for homeowners and developers alike. We understand that navigating planning regulations can be daunting, and our goal is to provide expert, clear, and efficient solutions.

With years of experience in the field, our team, led by **Adam Pegley** (Consultant) and **David Pegley** (Manager), is dedicated to providing tailored advice and comprehensive support. We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail, proactive communication, and a deep understanding of local planning policies.

Whether you're looking for initial **pre-planning advice**, need assistance with a complex **planning permission application**, or require a **Certificate of Lawfulness**, PP-Planning is here to guide you every step of the way. We believe in building strong relationships with our clients, ensuring their visions are realized within the framework of planning law.
